Overview

While many people think that retail theft is solely an impulsive, spur-of-the-moment act, it actually involes much more decision-making than they realize. Each act of theft behins with a choice that, in turn, is influenced by environment, and risk. We want to inform shop owners, and shoppers about the effects of retail theft.

Retail theft is an ever-evoling issue that has affected many store owners and shoppers for years, impacting financial and functional aspects of shops.

The Data

Shop owners have taken initative to implement solutions to combat shoplifting, showing promising results across the board.
